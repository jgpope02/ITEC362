Many people order a set of 4 tires even when just one needs to be replaced. However, the problem arises that while they are waiting for their set of
tires to be delivered and/or installed, they may feel unsafe driving on the worn tire. This is where Tires in Time comes in.At Tires in Time, we allow
customers select and rent a tire they want while their personal set comes in. This allows you to feel safe while waiting on your new set to come in.
We rent out tires based on either a flat rate for one whole week, or a price per mile rate. Using these two options, you can choose if you would rather
rent out a tire for one whole week, or if you don't plan to travel far on the rental tire, you can choose the price per mile plan.
